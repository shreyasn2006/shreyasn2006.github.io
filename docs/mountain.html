    A NEW KIND OF CHALLENGE IN THE FITBIT APP

    Start exploring unique locations by completing a different step goal during each day of this multi-day, solo challenge. From famous trails to iconic landmarks, you won’t believe where your steps can take you.

    Follow Trails

    Use your steps in real life to reach Daily Destinations and make your way along stunning virtual trails like Valley Loop in Yosemite.

    Find Landmarks

    From jaw-dropping peaks to stunning vistas, you’ll unlock 180º views of each destination & experience the unforgettable sights each landmark has to offer.

    Collect Treasures

    Along the way, you’ll uncover & collect hidden treasures like fun facts about your Adventure, interesting health info and mini fitness challenges.
